# Credit Card Customer Analytics

## Overview

An end-to-end **Data Analytics project** focused on understanding customer behavior, credit usage patterns, and attrition risk within a credit card portfolio.

Using **SQL Server, Excel Power Query, DAX, and Power BI**, the project explores customer demographics, spending behavior, credit utilization, and churn-related trends through interactive dashboards.

---

## Business Objective

Financial institutions need to identify customer segments with higher attrition risk and understand behavioral patterns that influence retention.

This project helps answer questions such as:

* Which customer groups are more likely to churn?
* How does attrition vary across age, income, and card categories?
* What differences exist between attrited and existing customers?
* How do credit limits and utilization vary across customer segments?

---

## Dataset

* Source: Kaggle Credit Card Customer Dataset

## Key Findings

* Attrition patterns vary across age, income, and card categories.
* Customers with lower transaction activity show higher churn tendencies.
* Credit utilization differs significantly across customer segments.
* Customer demographics provide valuable indicators for retention strategies.

## Business Recommendations

* Prioritize retention initiatives for customer segments with elevated attrition risk.
* Monitor customer activity levels as a leading indicator of churn.
* Develop targeted engagement campaigns for low-activity customers.
* Use customer segmentation to design personalized retention strategies.
